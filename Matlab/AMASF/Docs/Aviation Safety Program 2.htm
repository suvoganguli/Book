SVD

(System Vulnerability Detection)

 

 

Technologies for SVD will demonstrate and deliver a capability for data collection, integration, and analysis integration and leveraging of existing industry activities for enhanced discovery, identification, and evaluation of tactical and strategic safety risks in the NAS. In addition, security of airspace operations will be enhanced by providing ground controllers with an automated capability to identify an errant aircraft, and establish architectures for coordination of situational awareness among appropriate aviation security authorities in the management of securing the errant aircraft. The SVD Project will leverage existing technologies to demonstrate and deliver a system to accept and analyze large amounts of data and provide security interpretations in real-time to identify emerging trends of concern. Create and establish a data collection system for confidential, non-punitive reporting on aviation security through replication of the methodology, process, and philosophy used in the Aviation Safety Reporting System (ASRS). In addition, the SVD Project will leverage existing technologies to demonstrate and deliver a biosensor and a chemical sensor for use onboard an aircraft.
